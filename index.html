About me

I'm Piyush N Chandre — a full-stack developer and product-minded designer. I build accessible, high-performance web applications and tools that help teams move faster and users accomplish real goals.

My usual stack includes modern JavaScript (ES2020+), React/Next.js on the front end, Node.js/Express and PostgreSQL on the back end, and lightweight serverless functions where appropriate. I care about semantic HTML, CSS architecture, and shipping performant, well-tested features.

My approach centers on three things: usability, performance, and clear communication. I start with accessible components, add measured performance budgets, and write concise documentation and handoffs so designs and engineers stay aligned.

Open to freelance and full-time opportunities. If you'd like to collaborate on product design, engineering, or tooling, drop me a line at piyushchandre2004@gmail.com.
